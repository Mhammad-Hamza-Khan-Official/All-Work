# ServiceHub: Customer Flow Design Plan

## 1. Create Job Page (Mobile)
A multi-step form focused on clarity and ease of use.
- **Step 1: The Foundation:** Title, category selection, and description.
- **Step 2: Logistics:** Budget range slider and a map picker for the service location.
- **Step 3: Visual Context:** Drag-and-drop area for project photos.
- **Final Step:** Review and Publish.

## 2. My Jobs Page (Mobile)
A central hub for customers to track all their service requests.
- **Status Filtering:** Tabs for 'All', 'Open', 'In-Progress', and 'Completed'.
- **Job Cards:** High-level summary of each job, including title, date, budget, and a status badge.
- **Empty State:** Encouragement to post their first job if the list is empty.

## 3. Job Details Page (Mobile)
A deep dive into a specific project.
- **Project Brief:** Full description and visual gallery.
- **Applicant Tracking:** A dedicated section showing profiles of workers who have applied/bid.
- **Timeline/Status:** A visual progress tracker showing the job's journey from 'Posted' to 'Completed'.

## 4. Worker Listing / Discovery Page (Mobile)
Finding the right pro for the job.
- **Search & Filter:** Search bar for specific skills and filters for rating, distance, and price.
- **Worker Cards:** Highlights the worker's rating, "Verified Pro" status, starting rate, and core expertise.
- **Map View Toggle:** A way to see workers on an interactive map.
